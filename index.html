ABOUT

CyberPunks is world's first blockchain-powered cyberpunk-themed social and gaming metaverse, as well as the first slash to earn arena fight game.

You can battle like a ninja god in this fast-paced lightning-fast action cyberpunk-style virtual world. Each in-game character would be equipped with two high-tech swords to fight for game missions or among each other in team mode

CyberPunks Genesis NFT is a collection of 10,000 hand-drawn cyberpunk-style art on the Ethereum blockchain. It would be the entry ticket to access the cyberpunk gaming metaverse to enjoy a unique and cool first-person multi-player science-fiction game experience
